About

I am a University of Waterloo student studying Global Business and Digital Arts with a minor in Economics. My work focuses on visual communication, branding, and digital experiences that balance creativity with structure and intention.

I am particularly interested in how design can clarify complex ideas and create approachable, user centered systems. My background allows me to think both visually and strategically, considering how design decisions support broader business and communication goals.

Across my projects, I prioritize thoughtful typography, cohesive visual systems, and restrained color palettes to create work that feels polished, functional, and adaptable across platforms.

Attending the Blend Conference in Stratford in 2024 reinforced my interest in design as a tool for meaningful impact. I aim to contribute to projects that prioritize accessibility, community improvement, and thoughtful interaction, creating work that supports real people and real needs.
